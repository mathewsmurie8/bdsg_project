What is the role of BDSG?

BDSG is a platform that allows would be donors to get all information they need in matters pertaining to blood donation.

This platform provides all the information you need as a donor to be empowered to donate blood conveniently. BDSG leverages powerful technologies such as geolocation, SMS and our network of partner donation centers to allow you book and appointment and make a blood donation.
